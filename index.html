Privacy Policy

Extension Name: Open Hidden Links in New Tab

Effective Date: May 31, 2025

Thank you for using Open Hidden Links in New Tab (the "Extension"). This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information.

Information We Do Not Collect

The Extension is designed to enhance your browsing experience by allowing you to open links in new tabs. We affirm that:

  • No Personal Information: We do not collect, store, use, or share any personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email address, IP address, or location.
  • No Browsing Data: The Extension does not track your browsing history, websites visited, search queries, or any other online activity.
  • No Usage Statistics: We do not collect anonymous usage statistics or analytics about how you interact with the Extension.
  • Local Operation: All functionality of the Extension is performed locally within your browser. No data is transmitted to our servers or any third-party servers.

Permissions

The Extension requires the following browser permissions to function correctly:

  • contextMenus: This permission is used to add the "Open in new tab" option to the right-click context menu.
  • activeTab: This permission allows the Extension to interact with the content of the currently active tab when the context menu option is invoked. This is necessary to identify the link or element you wish to open.
  • tabs: This permission is used to open new browser tabs as requested by you through the Extension's functionality.

These permissions are requested solely for the purpose of providing the core features of the Extension. We do not use these permissions to access, collect, or transmit any of your data for other purposes.

Data Security

Since we do not collect or store any of your data, there is no user data to secure on our end. The Extension operates entirely within your browser's sandboxed environment, relying on the security measures provided by your web browser.

Third-Party Services

The Extension does not integrate with or rely on any third-party services for its core functionality, and therefore does not share any information with third parties.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time if the Extension's functionality changes in a way that impacts data handling. Any changes will be reflected in an updated version of this policy, and the effective date will be revised. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.
